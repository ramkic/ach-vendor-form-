TO BEGIN ACH PAYMENTS

1. Scan and create a PDF file of a voided check or deposit ticket to be attached to this form.

2. After completing all the required fields on this form:

a. Attach a voided check for a checking account or deposit slip for a savings account by clicking on the "Add Attachment" link at the bottom of this form. Your ACH direct deposit will begin after a successful pre-note test has occurred through the banking system.

b. Click on the Submit button to initiate the digital signature process.

c. When you receive an email from Eforms IRWD, click on the blue "Review and Sign" button in the body of the email to digitally sign and submit your ACH enrollment form. Your ACH enrollment form will not be processed until this step is completed.
